Explore our state-of-the-art facilities and vibrant campuses. Tours run throughout the Open Day, starting every 30 minutes from the Main Reception at each location.

City Campus Wolverhampton

Our main campus located in the heart of Wolverhampton city center. This vibrant campus houses most of our academic schools and is home to our state-of-the-art facilities including the Science Centre and Business School.

Key Facilities:

  • Harrison Learning Centre
  • Science Centre
  • Business School
  • Student Union
  • Sports Centre
  • City Campus Gym

Walsall Campus

Home to our sports facilities and School of Education, the Walsall Campus offers modern teaching facilities and a friendly, supportive environment. The campus features specialist sports facilities including an indoor sports hall, fitness suite, and all-weather pitches.

Key Facilities:

  • Performance Hub
  • Sports Complex
  • School of Education
  • Student Village
  • Learning Centre
  • all-Weather Pitches

Telford Innovation Campus

Our innovation campus focused on engineering and business, providing a cutting-edge environment for students interested in technology and innovation. The campus features specialist engineering facilities and strong links with industry partners.

Key Facilities:

  • Engineering Labs
  • Innovation Hub
